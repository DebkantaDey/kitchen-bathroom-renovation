Professional Stove Fitting

Home Professional Stove Fitting

The team at Eddie Comerford Building Contractors are HETAS trained wet and dry stove installers and also twin wall flue installer, and also Oftec registered solid fuel techincian, meaning it is a recognised fact that we have the required skills and expertise to install your new stove in your home. From our base in Nurney, Co. Carlow, we have fitted stoves across the county and surrounding areas.

Professional Stove Fitting

So you’ve bought a new stove that you adore and will be the centrepiece of whatever room you choose to put it in. Why not hire the experts to fit it for you? We do everything required to get your beautiful stove up and running, from fitting piping to building a chimney for the stove if necessary.

Install all makes and models of stoves in both the wet and dry variety including professional twin wall flue installers.

Stove Repair & Maintenance

Eddie Comerford Building Contractors also offer a stove repair and maintenance service. If your stove is not functioning correctly and you’re not sure what the issue may be, ­get in touch today and we will be glad to assess the situation and offer repair solutions.
Just as important as addressing repairs in a stove is stove maintenance. Regular maintenance of your stove can go a long way in ensuring that it is working efficiently and to the best of its ability. It may even prevent the need for major repairs in the future.
