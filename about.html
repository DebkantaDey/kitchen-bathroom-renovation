Your Reliable & Local Irish Business

Welcome to Eddie Comerford Building Contractors, your reliable and experienced partner for all your building needs. We specialize in stove installation, repairs, and fittings, bathroom installation, renovation, and remodeling, as well as kitchen planning and refit services.

With years of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as a leading contractor in the field, providing top-quality services to our clients. Our team of skilled professionals is committed to delivering exceptional results, using only the best materials and techniques to ensure your project is completed to the highest standards.

We understand that every client has unique needs and requirements, which is why we work closely with you to understand your vision and tailor our services to meet your specific needs. Our goal is to exceed your expectations and deliver a finished project that is both functional and aesthetically pleasing.

In addition to our stove, bathroom, and kitchen services, we also offer a range of construction activities such as house renovation and extensions. Our experienced team can help you transform your home into the space of your dreams, whether you're looking to add an extension, remodel your existing space, or make repairs.

At Eddie Comerford Building Contractors, we pride ourselves on our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction. We believe in building long-lasting relationships with our clients and providing exceptional service from start to finish. Contact us today to discuss your project and let us help you bring your vision to life.
