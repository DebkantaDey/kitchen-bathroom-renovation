Complete Turnkey Service

Eddie Comerford Building Contractors offer a complete turnkey construction service. This means that whether you are building a new home or creating new space through an extension, we can offer you a complete service from design through to completion. Whatever needs to be done to make the job perfect, we can take care of it.


From initial bricklaying though to windows, roofs and even socket placement, this extremely convenient service saves you from having to shop around several contractors to get the best deal because we do it all for you!
